Places Covered in Ujjain Tour

We cover all major temples and spiritual spots.

  • Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga

    One of the 12 Jyotirlingas, famous for Bhasma Aarti.

  • Ram Ghat

    Holy ghat on Kshipra river, famous for evening Aarti.

  • Harsiddhi Mata Temple

    One of the Shaktipeeths with deep lamps towers.

  • Kaal Bhairav

    The unique temple where liquor is offered as prasad.

  • Sandipani Ashram

    The place where Lord Krishna completed his education.

  • Mangalnath Temple

    Regarded as the birthplace of Mars (Mangal Graha).

Frequently Asked Questions

Your questions answered. Plan your spiritual journey with confidence.

Booking & Payments

How do I book a tour package?

You can book by filling out the enquiry form on our website, calling us directly, or sending a message on WhatsApp. We require a 20% advance payment to confirm your booking.

What payment modes are accepted?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe), Bank Transfers (IMPS/NEFT), and Cash payments upon arrival for the remaining balance.

Is there a cancellation policy?

Yes. Cancellations made 7 days prior to the trip date attract a 10% deduction. Cancellations within 7 days of the trip attract a 50% deduction. No refunds for last-minute cancellations (24 hours prior).

Travel & Darshan

Is VIP Darshan included in the package?

VIP Darshan tickets (Shighra Darshan) are generally not included in the standard package price unless specified (like in our Premium Package). However, we will assist you in booking these tickets online or at the counter.

Can we customize the itinerary?

Absolutely! All our packages are customizable. If you want to add a place (like Dewas or Maheshwar) or skip one, just let us know during booking, and we will adjust the cost accordingly.

Are Bhasma Aarti tickets guaranteed?

Bhasma Aarti booking is an online process governed by the temple trust and is subject to availability/lottery. We assist you with the process, but we cannot guarantee confirmed tickets as it is not in our control.

Stay & Facilities

What type of hotels do you provide?

We provide 3-star standard hotels for regular packages and 4-star premium hotels for our Premium packages. All hotels are hygienic, safe, and family-friendly with AC rooms.

Are meals included?

Breakfast is included in most overnight packages. Dinner is included in specific packages (like Malwa or Premium). Lunch is usually not included to give you flexibility to try local street food.
